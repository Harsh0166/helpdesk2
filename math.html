  • Set Identities -
    1. Identity law -
       i. A⋃∅ = A
      ii. A⋂U = A
    2. Dominartion law -
      i. A⋃U = U
      ii. A⋂∅ = ∅
    3. Idempotent law -
      i. A⋃A = A
      ii. A⋂A = A
    4. Double Complement law -
      i. (Ac)c = A
    5. Commutative law -
      i. A⋃B =B⋃A
      ii. A⋂B = B⋂A
    6. Associative law -
      i. A⋃(B⋃C) = (A⋃B)⋃C
      ii. A⋂(B⋂C) = (A⋂B)⋂C
    7. Distributive law -
      i. A⋃(B⋂C) = (A⋃B)⋂(A⋃B)
      ii. A⋂(B⋃C) = (A⋂B)⋃(A⋂C)
    8. De-Morgans law-
      i. (A⋃B)c = Ac⋂Bc
      ii. (A⋂B)c = Ac⋃Bc
    9. Absorption law -
      i. A⋃(A⋂B) = A
      ii. A⋂(A⋃B) = A
    10. Complement law -
      i. A⋃Ac = U
      ii. A⋂Ac = ∅
